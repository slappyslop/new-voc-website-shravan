How To Join

  1. Create an account on this website if you haven't already. The first time you log in, you will receive an email, and must confirm your email address before you continue.
  2. Fill out the membership form.
  3. After completing the form, you will be redirected to the waiver. If you navigated away from the waiver before signing it, you can find it again here.
  4. After signing the waiver, bring your membership fee to the clubroom during Gear Hours. Membership is $50 for UBC students and $70 for everyone else. The VOC clubroom is located in the basement of the AMS Nest in room LL201, right beside the Grand Noodle Emporium:
