About the VOC


What is the VOC?

The Varsity Outdoor Club (VOC) is a University of British Columbia student club based in Vancouver, BC with interest in all human-powered outdoor recreation activities. Although it is run by UBC students, the club is open for anyone to join. VOC trips are member-run, and range from beginner-friendly to experts-only.


Membership

VOC memberships are active for a year, expiring on September 30th of the following school year. Membership cost $50 for students, and $70 for non-student (associate) members. Perks of VOC membership include:

  • Access to member-run trips
  • The ability to borrow gear for free
  • Access to our library of maps, guidebooks and other outdoor literature

To join the VOC, create an account on this website, fill out the membership form here, including signing the waiver, and then bring your membership fee in cash to the VOC clubroom during gear hours


Clubroom

The VOC clubroom is located in the basement of the AMS Nest in room LL201, right beside the Grand Noodle Emporium:

Anyone, member or otherwise, is welcome in the clubroom. Gear hours and other events happening in the clubroom can be found on the Clubroom Calendar. Additionally, the clubroom is often open around lunchtime on weekdays. Note that we do not offer gear rental outside of designated gear hours.
