UBC Varsity Outdoor Club

The Varsity Outdoor Club (VOC) is a student run club of the University of British Columbia (UBC) in Vancouver, Canada.

We are avid outdoors enthusiasts and enjoy hiking, rock climbing, mountaineering, back-country skiing, paddling, bike-touring and all self-propelled outdoor adventures.
