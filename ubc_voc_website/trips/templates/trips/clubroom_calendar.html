Gear Hour Info (Click to expand)
If you wish to rent gear or pay for your membership, stop by during the Gear Hours on the calendar
  • Check this schedule before they come in... gear hours change frequently!
  • If you are not able to make it during these times, you can email 'gear at ubc-voc.com' to arrange another time to come in, but there is no guarantee that we will be able to accomodate your request
  • Our quartermasters are volunteers, and often have to leave right at the end of gear hours. Try to bring the required deposit in cash, and arrive early enough to take out gear, fit boots, adjust ski bindings, etc.
  • If you need to contact the gear team, please email 'gear at ubc-voc.com' or one of our quartermasters. Due to high volume, we may not respond to Instagram DMs
